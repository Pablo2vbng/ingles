Improve Your Language Skills

The Top 3 Tech Tools for Learners

Are you learning a new language? If the answer is yes, you know it can be difficult. Sometimes, you study a lot of grammar but you can't remember new words. Don't worry! Technology is here to help us. Today, there are many amazing apps and tools. In this post, I will show you my three favourite tech tools that make learning a language more easy and fun.

1. Anki: The King of Flashcards

For remember vocabulary, nothing is better than flashcards. But paper flashcards are from the past. Anki is a free app for your computer and phone. It uses a system called "Spaced Repetition". This means the app shows you difficult words more often, and easy words less often. It is very intelligent! You can create your own flashcards with text, images, and sounds, or download decks that other people made. It's a fantastic tool for build a strong vocabulary.




2. Tandem: Talk with Native Speakers

You need to practice speaking, right? Tandem is the perfect solution. It's an app that connects you with native speakers from all over the world. You find someone who is learning your language, and you help each other. You can chat with text, send voice messages, or make video calls. It’s like have a friend in another country. Practicing with real people is the best way to improve your fluency and confidence.

3. Toucan: Learn While You Browse

This tool is very clever. Toucan is a free browser extension. When you install it, it changes some words on any website you visit into the language you are learning. For example, if you are reading a news article in English, you will see some words in Spanish or French. You learn new vocabulary in context, without study hard. It’s a very natural way to learn new words every day while you are just navigating the internet.

What about you? What are your favourite tech tools for learning languages? Share them in the comments below!
