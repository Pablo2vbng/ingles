Grammar Practice: Questions & Superlatives

Direct and Indirect Questions

Teoría

Preguntas Directas: Usan la estructura: Question Word + Auxiliary + Subject + Verb. (Ej: How does persuasion influence advertising?). Para respuestas de sí/no, empiezan con un auxiliar (Ej: Does this advert connect with the public?).

Preguntas Indirectas: Son más formales y educadas. Se introducen con frases como:

  • I’d like to know…
  • Can you tell me…?
  • Do you know…?
  • I was wondering…

Después de la frase introductoria, se usa el orden de una oración afirmativa (Sujeto + Verbo) y no se usa el auxiliar do/does/did. Para preguntas de sí/no, se añade if o whether.

  • Directa: How often does this advert appear?
  • Indirecta: Do you know how often this advert appears?
  • Directa: Is this campaign funded?
  • Indirecta: I was wondering if this campaign is funded.

Practice: Indirect Questions

1. Reescribe las siguientes preguntas directas como preguntas indirectas usando la frase de inicio.

  1. "Why has the digital revolution transformed advertising?"

  2. "Does this advert connect with the public?"
    Do you know
  3. "Did the advent of radio transform advertising?"
    I’d like to know
  4. "Are you interested in artificial intelligence?"
    I was wondering

  1. Can you / tell me / if / the Summer dresses / have / a 10% discount
  2. Do you know / if / you have the shoes / you advertised
  3. Would you mind / telling me / where / the toilets / are

Superlative Forms

Teoría

Los superlativos se usan para describir el máximo o mínimo de un rango.

  • Añade -est a adjetivos cortos (tall -> tallest).
  • Usa most con adjetivos largos (accurate -> most accurate).
  • Algunos son irregulares (good -> best, bad -> worst).
  • Normalmente se usa the antes del superlativo (This is the fastest car).

Practice: Superlatives

1. Completa los huecos con la forma superlativa correcta del adjetivo.

  1. The new iPhone is the smartphone on the market.
  2. This is the pizza I have ever eaten.
  3. The Eiffel Tower is the building in Paris.

2. Corrige los errores en el uso de los superlativos en estas oraciones.

  1. Our product is the goodest in the market.
  2. This laptop is the bestest choice for professionals.
  3. This car model is fastest in the whole dealership.
