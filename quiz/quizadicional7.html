Ejercicio 1: First & Second Conditionals

2. He ___ depressed if he fails the exam.

3. If I didn't know you, I ___ you were crazy.

4. Tell me the truth if you ___.

5. If you had a bigger salary, you ___ more unnecessary things.

6. If it ___ for Tom, I wouldn't be here.

7. You should call me if you ___ anything unusual.

8. If they had this player, they ___ a single match.

9. Our lives would be much easier if you ___ closer to home.

10. If I got a job in France, we ___ crêpes every day.

Ejercicio 2: Conditionals - Fill in the gaps

11. If I were you, I ___ (start) now.

12. If Charles ___ (ask) me, I would lend him my tools.

13. If I ___ (be) at home, I will study the new words.

14. If Sam has a hammer, he ___ (lend) it to me.

15. If Mary helped in the kitchen, she ___ (get) more pocket money.

16. If you ___ (come), you would see her.

17. If we go to London, we ___ (see) the Tower.

18. Susan will help you if she ___ (get) more pocket money.

19. I ___ (tell) you if you asked me.

20. He ___ (wash) his hands if you give him some soap.
