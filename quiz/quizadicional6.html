Grammar Quiz: Questions & Comparisons

Recordatorio de Gramática:

  • Direct Questions: Usan un orden de palabras invertido (verbo auxiliar + sujeto). (e.g., Where is he?)
  • Indirect Questions: Van después de una frase introductoria y usan el orden de una oración normal (sujeto + verbo). (e.g., I don't know where he is.)
  • Comparatives: Comparan dos cosas (e.g., taller, more expensive).
  • Superlatives: Muestran el grado más alto de un grupo (e.g., the tallest, the most expensive).

Ejercicio 1: Direct and Indirect Questions

Elige la forma correcta para completar cada pregunta.

1. I don't know the answer. How many times … to tell you?

2. I don't know … so unhappy.

3. Do you have any idea when …?

4. How many people … for the party?

5. I wonder ….

6. Did she tell you how often …?

7. I'd like to know … for the experiment.

8. Could you tell me why …?

9. Can I ask you how long … here?

10. Why …?

Ejercicio 2: Comparatives and Superlatives

Elige la respuesta correcta.

1. This building is … that building.

2. Hassan and Youssef are … Ian.

3. The Pacific Ocean … the Atlantic Ocean.

4. Serena and Lizzie are short, but Sue is ….

5. We are all eight, but Gary is ….

6. Of the three cars, the red one is ….

7. Chloe is older than Polly, but Frank is ….
